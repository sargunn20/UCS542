Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Is there a registration fee?

No, participation is free for all college students. You just need your student ID.

Can I participate in multiple events?

Yes, you can register for multiple events, but please check the schedule to avoid timing clashes.

Is accommodation provided?

Accommodation is not provided by the college. Participants are requested to arrange for their own stay.
